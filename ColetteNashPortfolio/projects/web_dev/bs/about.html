About Us

We are open Monday to Saturday 9am - 6pm

Our Independent Irish Book Store is located in the Dungarvan Shopping Centre, Dungarvan, Co Waterford, Ireland. We have been in business since 1971 and we are a family owned and run local Irish Company. Our store offers a large range of books, from fiction and non fiction to books on the 1916 Rising and children's books.
